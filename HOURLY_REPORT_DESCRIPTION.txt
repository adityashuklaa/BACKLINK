HOURLY REPORT DESCRIPTION

What is this?
A simple tracker that records backlink submissions every hour (7 PM to 11 PM).

What do we track?
- How many backlinks we submitted
- How many were successful
- Success rate (%)
- Breakdown by strategy (Directories, Social, Forums, Guest Posts, Blog Comments)
- Any issues (rate-limits, CAPTCHAs, etc)
- Who was in charge

Why?
So leadership knows how much work we're doing every hour and if we're on track to meet daily targets.

How do we use it?
1. Every hour, fill in: submissions, successful, failed numbers
2. Calculate success rate
3. Paste into Excel sheet
4. At end of day, send to boss

Example for 7 PM:
- Submitted: 12 backlinks
- Successful: 9
- Failed: 3
- Success Rate: 75%
- Directories: 3, Social: 2, Forums: 1, Guest: 4, Blog: 1
