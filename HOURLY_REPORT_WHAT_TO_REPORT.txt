WHAT TO REPORT EVERY HOUR (For Excel Sheet)

During your working hours, track and report THESE 11 things every hour:

1. DATE
   What: Today's date
   Example: 2026-04-10

2. HOUR
   What: What hour are you reporting
   Example: 7 PM = 19:00, 8 PM = 20:00

3. TOTAL SUBMISSIONS
   What: How many backlinks did you try to submit this hour
   Example: 12 (you attempted 12 submissions)

4. SUCCESSFUL
   What: How many of those actually worked/got accepted
   Example: 9 (out of 12, 9 were successful)

5. FAILED
   What: How many did NOT work/got rejected
   Example: 3 (rejected or failed)

6. SUCCESS RATE
   What: Calculate = Successful ÷ Total × 100
   Example: 9 ÷ 12 × 100 = 75%

7. DIRECTORIES
   What: How many successful backlinks from directory sites (Yelp, BBB, etc)
   Example: 3

8. SOCIAL BOOKMARKING
   What: How many successful backlinks from social sites (Reddit, Diigo, etc)
   Example: 2

9. FORUM PROFILES
   What: How many successful backlinks from forum/profile sites
   Example: 1

10. GUEST POST OUTREACH
    What: How many successful backlinks from guest post emails sent/accepted
    Example: 4

11. BLOG COMMENTS
    What: How many successful backlinks from blog comment submissions
    Example: 2

12. ISSUES
    What: Any problems this hour? (rate-limits, CAPTCHAs, errors, etc)
    Example: "1 rate-limit on Yelp", or "None"

13. OWNER
    What: Your name / who worked this hour
    Example: Backend Engineer

SIMPLE VERSION - WHAT TO WRITE IN EXCEL EACH HOUR:

Date: 2026-04-10, Hour: 19:00, Submissions: 12, Successful: 9, Failed: 3, Success Rate: 75%, Directories: 3, Social: 2, Forums: 1, Guest: 4, Blog: 2, Issues: None, Owner: Your Name

That's it. Do this for 19:00, 20:00, 21:00, 22:00, 23:00 (7 PM to 11 PM)

Then paste all 5 lines into one Excel sheet.
