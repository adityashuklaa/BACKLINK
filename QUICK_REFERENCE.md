## 🚨 Red Flags (Auto-Pause System)

If ANY occur, pause submissions & escalate:

1. **429 Too Many Requests** on same domain >2x in 1 week
2. **Account Suspension** on 3+ platforms in 1 week
3. **Google Core Update** impact detected (rankings drop >20%)
4. **Legal Notice** or C&D from platform
5. **Domain Authority < 15** on >20% of submissions

**Action**: Stop → Audit → Report to Compliance Officer + Leadership
