### 3. Ethics & Compliance (New Framework)

**File**: `COMPLIANCE_CHECKLIST.md`

#### Mandatory Pre-Submission Checks
- ✅ Platform ToS validation (auto-reject spam destinations)
- ✅ Domain Authority filter (DA > 20 only)
- ✅ Link relevance checks (contextual matching)
- ✅ Duplicate prevention (max 1 per site per 30 days)
- ✅ Spam score validation (reject high-risk sites)

#### Per-Strategy Compliance Rules
- ✅ Directories: Only legitimate sites, 1 per quarter per domain
- ✅ Social: Rate limited to 1 post per platform per 48 hours
- ✅ Forums: Genuine profiles only, natural link placement
- ✅ Guest Posts: Only "write for us" pages, editorial review required
- ✅ Blog Comments: Substantive comments only, 2x per month max per blog

#### Red Flags (Auto-Halt)
- 429 errors on same domain >2x/week → pause & escalate
- Account suspension on 3+ platforms → audit IP & rotate
- Detection incidents >5/week → investigate stealth measures
- Domain reputation drop → halt all submissions pending review

**Outcome**: System has guardrails. No longer "spray & pray" spam risk. Compliant with platform ToS.
