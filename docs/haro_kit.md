Three patterns from running US/Canada SMB VoIP migrations in 2026:

Associate Agreements (medical practices), SOC 2 Type 2 audit
reports (any vendor selling into mid-market), PCI-DSS for
anyone taking payment over phone. SMBs underprice the vendor-
review hours these eat.

Happy to walk through the actual deployment data if useful —

across [ROUND NUMBER] customer transitions.
