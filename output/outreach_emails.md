## Suggested cadence

- **Week 1:** 10 gold-tier pitches per day (Template 1 for publications, Template 4 for aggregators)
- **Week 2:** 5 guest post pitches per day (Template 3)
- **Ongoing:** When you or the team spot a brand mention without a link, send Template 2

**Expected response rates** (based on cold-but-targeted outreach to real industry contacts):
- Template 1 (inclusion in reviews): 5-15% response → 3-10 editorial placements/month
- Template 2 (unlinked mention): 20-40% (if mention exists) → depends on how many mentions exist
- Template 3 (guest post): 10-20% response → 2-5 placements/month
- Template 4 (aggregator listing): 30-60% (easier, it's their job to add listings) → 15-20 listings per month

**Conservative month-1 forecast:** 20-40 real editorial backlinks across the 50 targets listed.
