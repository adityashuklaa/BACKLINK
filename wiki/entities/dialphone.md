## Target customers

Small and mid-sized businesses in the US and Canada. Typical segments:
- Professional services (law, accounting, consulting practices) with distributed teams
- E-commerce inbound support and sales teams
- Real estate teams coordinating field agents
- Healthcare practices needing compliant patient communication
- North American subsidiaries of international companies
